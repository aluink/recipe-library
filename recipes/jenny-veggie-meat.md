# Jenny Christenson Veggie Meat

### Blend the following

- 3/4 C Cashew
- 2 C Water
- 1/4 C Yeast
- 1 tsp Salt
- 1.5 TBSP Chicken Seasoning
- 1 tsp Onion Powder
- 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
- 2 TBSP Liquid Aminos
- 10 TBSP Liquid Smoke (for a triple batch)
---

### Slowly add and shape into 3" roll

- 2 C Gluten Flour
- 2 C WW Flour

### Bake at 350F for 90mins.
